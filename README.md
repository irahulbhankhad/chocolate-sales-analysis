# 🍫 Chocolate Sales Analytics  
### Strategic Revenue & Supply Chain Analysis

---

## 📊 Project Overview
This project performs an end-to-end analysis of chocolate sales data to uncover key business insights related to revenue, product demand, growth trends, and sales performance.

The goal is to transform raw transactional data into actionable insights that can support strategic decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall sales performance
- Identify top-performing products and countries
- Evaluate salesperson efficiency
- Understand revenue vs volume relationship
- Detect outliers and abnormal transactions
- Discover high-growth markets

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **Python**
- **Pandas**
- **Plotly (Interactive Visualizations)**

---

## 📂 Dataset Features
- Sales Person  
- Country  
- Product  
- Date  
- Amount  
- Boxes Shipped  

---

## 📈 Key Analysis Performed

### 🔹 Descriptive Analytics
- Revenue distribution across products and countries
- Top-performing salespersons

### 🔹 Intermediate Analysis
- Monthly and yearly growth trends
- Product demand distribution

### 🔹 Advanced Business Insights
- Revenue per box (efficiency analysis)
- Salesperson ranking system
- Revenue vs volume relationship
- Country-product heatmap analysis

### 🔹 Statistical Analysis
- Outlier detection using IQR
- Consistency analysis using variance

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- A small number of products contribute to the majority of shipments  
- Revenue is not always directly proportional to volume  
- Some salespersons are highly efficient despite lower volume  
- Certain country-product combinations dominate revenue  
- Growth trends highlight emerging high-potential markets  

---

## 💡 Business Impact

This analysis can help businesses:
- Optimize product strategy  
- Improve sales efficiency  
- Identify high-growth markets  
- Enhance decision-making using data-driven insights
